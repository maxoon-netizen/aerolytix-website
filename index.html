Frequently Asked Questions

What do you need from me to start?
Farm location (or coordinates), approximate field size, crop type, and the decision you want to support (counting, weeds, water, monitoring). If you have target dates (e.g. specific growth stage), include them.
How accurate are the plant counts?
Accuracy depends on crop stage, canopy closure, and image resolution. We typically see 95%+ accuracy for row crops pre-canopy closure. We’ll recommend the right timing.
Can you deliver files for GIS / farm platforms?
Yes. We deliver PDF for sharing, plus geospatial formats like GeoTIFF and shapefiles so you can load layers directly.
